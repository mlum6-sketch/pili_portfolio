ʻĀina Momona Campaign

A visual identity campaign celebrating abundance, stewardship, and aloha ʻāina.

ʻĀina Momona represents richness — not in wealth, but in wellness, community, and relationship to ʻāina. This project uses earthy tones, organic shapes, and grounded typography to communicate that sense of fullness.

My goal was to design branding that feels rooted, intentional, and culturally respectful while still being modern and clean.

Process

  • Medium: Digital branding + campaign identity
  • Tools: Illustrator, Photoshop
  • Themes: Abundance, stewardship, community
