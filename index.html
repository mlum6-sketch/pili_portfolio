ʻĀina Momona Campaign

A visual identity campaign rooted in abundance, culture, and the relationship between people and ʻāina.

ʻĀina Momona represents the fullness and richness of land when it is cared for through kuleana, aloha ʻāina, and generational stewardship. This project explores how visual storytelling can help communicate those values through design choices—such as earthy tones, organic shapes, and grounded typography.

My intention was to create a campaign that feels warm, intentional, and connected to place. The branding blends traditional patterns with a clean contemporary aesthetic to communicate abundance in a way that feels culturally respectful and visually compelling.

Process & Details

  • Medium: Digital branding, campaign design
  • Tools: Illustrator, Photoshop
  • Themes: ʻĀina, abundance, stewardship, identity
